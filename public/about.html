Fun and Interesting Nutrition Articles

Nutrition 101close

Whatever diet you chooose and stick with is the diet that works. There are hundreds of different diet fads out. If you are trying to choose one, choose the one that you know you will stick with.

Just know everything you ingest initiates a chemical reaction in your body. That chemical reaction always has an output. Sometimes understanding what you are eating helps you understand how you are feeling.

Nerd Fitness

Exerciseclose

Exercising is a strong word. People burn calories just sitting watching TV. If you are trying to lose weight, or gain muscle, diet will be 80% of that process.

Weightlifting, Cardio, or any kind of sport, the verdict is out, each have their own benefits but as for burning calories they all have about the same effect. A healthy mix of all if better for your muscles.

Any kind of work out plan should start with a focus on injury prevention

Article: Your Brain on Food

Wellnessclose

In general, you should try and be good to yourself. It helps your mood, your outlook on life, and how you interactive with the world. If you are trying any diet, having a good mental state helps you stick with it.

Stress can cause your body to store fat more easily as well as hold on to your fat stores more strongly. Stress also leads to some serious health conditions if prolonged. Check out this article for more information!
